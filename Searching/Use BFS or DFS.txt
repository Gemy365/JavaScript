Question: If you know a solution is not far from the root of the tree:
Use: BFS

Question: If the tree is very deep and solutions are rare, 
Use: BFS (DFS will take long time. )

Question: If the tree is very wide:
Use: DFS (BFS will need too much memory)

Question: If solutions are frequent but located deep in the tree
Use: DFS

Question: determining whether a path exists between two nodes
Use: DFS

Question: Finding the shortest path
Use: BFS
